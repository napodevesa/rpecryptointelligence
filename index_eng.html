Mission and goals

RPE is a company whose mission is to achieve incremental and continuous capitalization over time for all its clients, as well as for the company itself.

We propose systematic savings as the fundamental basis for the growth of assets. For this objective, we offer the necessary tech tools so that our clients can save in strong currency and channel part of their income in the virtuous circle of:

✔ Saving ✔ Investment

We encourage recurring investment of savings as well as the benefits and dividends obtained from the investments themselves, thus enhancing the effect of compound interest.

Our company manages its own investment funds, both in stocks and indices, dynamically and periodically. The investor will be able to exactly copy our investment portfolio.
